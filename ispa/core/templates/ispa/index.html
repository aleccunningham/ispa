Illinois Sports Buisiness Association

The mission of the SBA is to offer students that are interested in pursuing a career in sports with opportunities to expand their personal and professional competencies within the sports industry through insightful guest speakers, workshops, and professional conferences. Our organization is headed by dedicated students looking to better the University of Illinois as a whole.

Past Events

Networking and attending events has been one of the most valuable service we've offered our members; trips to the NCAA headquarters, Speakers and Games are all part of our mission

Join Us
  • September 15, 2016 Chicago Cubs

    Our members got the unique opportunity to sit in on the third baseline during batting practice and talk to some of the Cubs front office staff. Following our discussion, we stayed and enjoyed the game.

  • April 11, 2017 Chicago Bears

    At SBA traveled to Soldier Field for a tour of the stadium and a chat with Bears employees. The Bears gave use an in depth tour of the stadium, including a look inside the locker rooms, a trip down onto the field and a peek into some of the top  notch suites. Our panel discussion included around eleven Bears full time across several departments.

  • December 20, 2016 Chicago Bears & Blackhawks

    We had the opportunity to visit the United Center and hear from several executives from the Chicago Bulls and Chicago Blackhawks front offices, as well as some of the United Center staff. During lunch, we were able to meet and network with some of the Illinois alumni that work for both organizations. We ended the day with a tour of the stadium/

  • April 25, 2017 Chicago White Sox
